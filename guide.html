Reversy Guide

Cars, upgrades, chickens, hazard walkers, mother-hen eggs, and boss rules for Uncrossy Road. All run-shop upgrades are temporary, and cash, upgrades, and eagle uses reset after game over.

Doomscroller About 1 in 12 spawns after 2:00 1 126 $0 0 Ignores mud. If traffic hits one, you lose $10 and it drops a goofy scrolling phone. If it safely crosses, you gain $10.
Mother Hen In pairs behind bruisers after 3:00 2 96 $18 1 Brown hen that hurls a comically large egg every 4 seconds. The egg can destroy a small car or truck, and it makes buses or the sky eagle swerve into another lane.
Boss Chicken At 2:30 20 118 x 0.125 $48 5 Clears the road when it arrives, ignores mud, resists one-tap effects, and costs 5 escapes if it crosses.

Shielded birds can start appearing after 1:30. They are rare at first, ramp up over time, and their body color shows the current layer: green, blue, purple, then red.
